California Fire Map and Visualizations



Fire Map

Below, you will see a map of California with fire icons. Each fire icon represents where a fire has taken place, or is currently taken place. You can click on each fire icon to see the following details: fire name, county, start date, extinguish date, acreage, and a link to more information.


Additionally, on the top right corner of the map, you will see a control box. If you hover your mouse over it, you will notice that the years between 2016 and 2020 will appear with checkboxes. Check the box for each year that you would like displayed on the map.


Visualizations

To help understand how devastating these fires are, we have provided three chart visualizations: fires by county, acreage burned by county and total acres burned per year. Click on them for a more in-depth and interactive analysis.
