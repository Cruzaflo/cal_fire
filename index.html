California Fire Map and Visualizations



Fire Map

Below, you will see a map of California with fire icons. Each fire icon represents where a fire has taken place, or is currently taken place. You can click on each fire icon to see the following details: fire name, county, start date, extinguish date, acreage, and a link to more information


The map also containes a control box on the top right. If you hover your mouse over it, you will notice that the years between 2016 and 2020 will appear with checkboxes. Check the box for each year that you would like displayed on the map.


Visualizations

To help understand how devastating these fires are, we have provided two chart visualizations: fires by county and acreage burned by county. Click on them for a more in-depth and interactive analysis.
